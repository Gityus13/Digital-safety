Task 01 — Digital Safety

The S.M.A.R.T
Rules to Stay Safe

Five easy rules to help you be safe on the web. Move to the right to see each rule. They help you and your friends stay safe.

Move right to see →

01 / 05
🔒
S — Stay Safe

Keep Your Name a Secret

Do not tell people your name, where you live, or your password. Keep your info safe, just like you keep your toys safe.

S
02 / 05
🚫
M — Don't Meet

Only Meet People You Know

Never go to see someone you only met on the web. Tell a grown-up if someone asks to meet. Stay safe with people you know in real life.

M
03 / 05
⚠️
A — Don't Accept

Say No to Strange Things

Do not open files or links from people you don't know. They can hurt your computer. If you don't know who sent it, do not click.

A
04 / 05
🔍
R — Be Reliable

Check If It's True

Some things on the web are not true. Ask a grown-up or check in a book. Do not share things until you know they are real.

R
05 / 05
💬
T — Tell Someone

Talk to a Grown-Up

If something makes you feel sad, scared, or weird, tell a grown-up you trust. They can help you. You did nothing wrong by telling.

T
Task 02 — Cell Networks

How Your Voice
Goes Far Away

01
Step 1 · Your Phone

Your Voice Turns Into Tiny Bits

When you talk, your phone changes your voice into tiny bits of data. It sends them out as waves, like a radio.

02
Step 2 · The Tower

A Tall Tower Hears You

A big tall tower catches the waves from your phone. The tower is like a helper that sends your voice to the right place.

03
Step 3 · Fast Wires

Fast Wires Carry Your Voice

Your voice travels through tiny glass wires. The wires are under the ground and under the sea. They are super fast!

04
Step 4 · Far Away Places

Across the Ocean

If your friend is far away, your voice goes under the ocean in big cables. Some voices go to space and back on satellites!

05
Step 5 · Your Friend's Phone

Your Friend Hears You!

Your voice goes to a tower near your friend. The tower sends it to their phone. They hear you in less than a blink!

Places Where
Phones Don't Work

🏔️
Big Mountains
In tall mountains, phones cannot find a tower. The rocks block the waves.
💰
Too Much Money
Putting towers in far places costs a lot of money. Some towns do not have them yet.
⚡
No Power
Towers need power to work. Some places use the sun to make power for towers.
🛰️
Space Helpers
New helpers in space can give the web to places with no towers. They are called satellites.
Task 02 — Global Impact

New Tech Helps
People Far Away

A True Story · West Africa

Paying With Your Phone

In some places, there are no banks. People use their phones like a wallet. They can pay, save, and send money to family. It helps so much!

0
People use phone money instead of a bank
Good Things

What Helps People

  • →People can save money on their phone
  • →Farmers get paid right away for their food
  • →You can pay for a doctor with your phone
  • →Small shops can borrow money to grow
Hard Things

What Can Go Wrong

  • →Bad people try to trick you and take money
  • →If power goes out, phones stop working
  • →Companies know a lot about you

Task 01 — What To Do

Your Safe
Helper Kit

Know what to do when things feel wrong. Be fast. Stay calm. Tell a grown-up.

01

Something Made You Feel Weird

Stop looking at it. Do not answer. Take a picture of it. Tell a grown-up. You did the right thing!

Get Help ↗
02

You Got a Strange File

Do not open it. Turn off the web. Tell a grown-up. Change your passwords. Be safe!

03
